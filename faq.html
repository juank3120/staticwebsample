Frequently Asked Questions

What are capybaras?

Capybaras are the largest rodents in the world, native to South America. They are social, gentle animals that live in groups near water bodies.

Why do capybaras need protection?

Capybaras face threats from habitat destruction, hunting, and human encroachment. Protecting their natural habitats and promoting conservation efforts are crucial for their survival.

How can I help protect capybaras?

You can help by supporting conservation organizations, spreading awareness, and advocating for policies that protect wildlife and their habitats.
